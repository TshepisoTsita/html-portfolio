The Best Movies According to Tshepiso

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sarafina

Three childhood friends ,reunite on graduation and promptly decide to take a trip across the country.One of my best childhood movie .Very Fun and Joyful

It's different groups of people who enjoy street-dance and Hip-hop.
